What I Do

I make better websites and write better copy than anyone else.

Be careful though... The more money you throw at me, the more your sales and subscriber numbers will explode.

I know, I know, it's dumb that I even have to warn people about this... but some people just don't like their numbers exploding!

In the words of my morbidly obese and clinically depressed uncle, their numbers are "fine just the way they are" and "there's nothing you can do to change them"!

So if you don't want your numbers exploding, you should probably hire someone else.

Otherwise, you should probably start throwing all of your money to me RIGHT NOW before someone else does.

Because for some reason, lots of rich people like to throw big numbers at me... like I'm some kind of expensive human calculator or something.

They throw big numbers at me and ask me to multiply them.

And I feel absolutely terrible about it because they've got the wrong idea... I'm no calculator, and I'm not even that great at math... I'm just a writer and website guy!

I can only multiply so many numbers, and I only know one thing about multiplication... the bigger the numbers, the bigger the result.

But I like big numbers too, so if you share in my enjoyment of big numbers, feel free to throw some at me!

Results guaranteed or your money back.
